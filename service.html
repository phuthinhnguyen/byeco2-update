Energy Auditing

Energy Auditing is the very first activity that is considered an integral part of business operations. However, due to their intrinsic complexity, the issue has not been systematically applied. In the context of climate change, energy-related activities would be an imperative in their strategic portfolio.

BYECO2 provides a package of energy audit services according to the size and scope of companies, including site assessment, preliminary energy audit, detailed audit, investment-grade audit.

Digitalized Energy Management

BYECO2 provide consultancy service on developing an ISO 5001:2018 Energy Management System managed by One-Platform where the owner and operators can visualize and monitor energy-related data via computer and mobile to make their informed decision-making convenient.

Feasibility Studies

BYECO2 to conduct bankable feasibility studies for energy efficiency improvement and renewable energy development projects. They cover technical and financial feasibility assessment of proposed solutions.

Digitalized Carbon Footprint Management

Making corporate GHG emission accounting and reporting to comply with the law and regulations.

Building up a smart exhaustive carbon footprint management for the whole supply chain that is transparent to any stakeholders.

Sustainability Transition

Integrating renewable energies into customers' facilities and building corporate green portfolios

Carbon Trading

Providing cost-effective strategies for reducing carbon emissions through flexible mechanisms in the upcoming domestic carbon market.

Making the corporate carbon assets available to international carbon market through Emission Trading Schemes.
